Welcome to the Guided Tour

You are listening to a live audio feed from your tour guide. Please keep your headphones on and follow the group.

Tips

- Use headphones for the best experience
- Keep your phone's screen off to save battery
- Audio will continue playing in the background
- Stay within WiFi range (~50 meters)

Tour map will be displayed here.

To add a map, place a map.jpg file
in the web folder on the Raspberry Pi.

How to Connect

  1. Connect to WiFi network "TourGuide" (password: listen123)
  2. Open this page (it should open automatically)
  3. Plug in your headphones or earbuds
  4. Tap the green Play button above
  5. Adjust volume to a comfortable level

Trouble?

No audio? Make sure headphones are plugged in and volume is up.

Audio cutting out? Move closer to the group — stay within ~50 meters.

Page not loading? Disconnect from mobile data and connect only to TourGuide WiFi.
